Privacy Policy — Instagram Carousel Maker

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This privacy notice for Caroxa — Instagram Carousel Maker ("we," "us," or "our") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Caroxa is the ultimate app for creating beautiful Instagram carousels, photo collages, stories, Reels covers, highlight covers, and grid layouts — all in one place, designed for creators, influencers, bloggers, and brands.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

Summary of Key Points

  • We collect device identifiers and purchase data primarily through RevenueCat to manage your subscription.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising.
  • Photos and designs you create are saved locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • All carousel layouts, collage templates, and design edits are processed entirely on-device.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18 years of age.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Data Collected Automatically

  • Device ID — Used by RevenueCat to identify your device for subscription management.
  • User ID — A RevenueCat-assigned identifier to link your account with your subscription status.
  • Purchase & Subscription History — Records of your Caroxa Pro subscription processed via Apple App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat.
  • Product Interaction Data — Anonymized data about how you use the app (e.g., templates used, features activated) to improve the platform.
  • Crash & Performance Data — Technical diagnostics including crash logs, launch times, and performance metrics to ensure app stability.

Data You Provide

  • Caroxa does not require account registration or login.
  • If you contact our support team, we may collect your email address and the content of your message solely to respond to your inquiry.

Photos & Designs

🖼 All photos, carousels, collages, and designs you create with Caroxa are saved directly to your device (Camera Roll / Photos library). We do not upload, access, or store your photos or design projects on our servers.

✨ All carousel layouts, collage templates, text overlays, filters, and feed previews are processed entirely on your device. No image data is transmitted to external servers during editing or creation.

Photo Library Access

Caroxa requests access to your device's photo library to let you import photos into carousels, collages, and stories. This permission is used exclusively for the core design functionality of the app. You may revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.

Payment Information

All payments are processed directly through the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not have access to your payment card details, bank information, or billing address. RevenueCat receives only confirmation of your subscription status.
